At a glanceplatform & principles

Platform & constraints

TargetPC desktop (Windows primary) · premium one-time purchase on Steam
CanvasDesigned at 1920×1080, 16:9 · scales to 1440p / 4K via UI-scale setting
InputKeyboard + mouse — hover, right-click context, drag & hotkey glyphs throughout
RendererGodot 4.6 Control nodes — panels, containers, sliders, dropdowns, tab bars, grids
IdentityDark, desaturated surfaces · faceted / chamfered chrome · thin cel-shade edge-light · flat color blocks
AccessibilityWCAG AA text on dark · UI scaling · colorblind-safe teams · never color alone (icon + label)

Faction colors

Reserved & colorblind-safe (Okabe-Ito derived). Distinct from the UI accent — every team also carries a glyph & label so meaning is never encoded by color alone.
