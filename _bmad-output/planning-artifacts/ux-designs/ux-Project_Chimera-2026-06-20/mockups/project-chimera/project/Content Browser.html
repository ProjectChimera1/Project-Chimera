About this scenario

Hold the tidal fortress against eight escalating waves of deep-sea raiders. Tidefall Bastion is a co-op survival map built for 1–8 commanders who share a single base economy — coordinate your wall upgrades, split the lanes, and bank Crystal for the final siege.

Features a custom tech tree of 14 defensive structures, three hero classes with branching ultimates, and a dynamic difficulty director that scales spawn pressure to your squad size. Every unit and trigger in this map was built in the Chimera Creation Suite — no external code.
